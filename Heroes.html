Top Movie Heroes

10. Aragorn
"The Lord of the Rings"

Opening this rating is a cult character not only of cinema, but also of the book worlds of writer John R. R. Tolkien – Aragorn. In the cinema, the role of the first king of the Reunited Kingdom of Gondor and Arnor was brilliantly played by Viggo Mortensen. His character appeared in the films: "The Lord of the Rings: The Fellowship of the Ring", "The Lord of the Rings: The Two Towers" and "The Lord of the Rings: The Return of the King".

9. Captain Jack Sparrow
"Pirates of the Caribbean"

Pirate, captain, eccentric, charming and never despairing – of course, this is Jack Sparrow. Apologies, Captain Jack Sparrow. Screenwriters Ted Elliott and Terry Rossio created Captain Jack in the spirit of characters from classic literature and opera, such as Figaro; comic, mischievous counterpoint to the film's heroes. In all five films of the "Pirates of the Caribbean" series, his role was played by Johnny Depp.

8. Iron Man
"Marvel Cinematic Universe"

Iron Man or if without any masks under the mask – Tony Stark. This superhero of "Marvel" comics immediately became loved by fans of cinema, as soon as they saw Robert Downey Jr. playing his role on the big screen. In addition to solo films, Iron Man also appeared in a series of superhero films: "The Avengers", "Spider-Man" and "The Incredible Hulk".

7. Marty McFly
"Back to the Future"

One could endlessly praise Michael J. Fox for his role in the film "Back to the Future", but the fact remains – his character Marty McFly is still considered one of the most cult characters in the history of cinema. The "Back to the Future" trilogy is still very captivating and interesting to watch even thirty-eight years after the release of the first film.

6. John McClane
"Die Hard"

If someone doesn't know, John McClane is a police detective leading a stubborn struggle against terrorists. This character of the series of action films "Die Hard" was played by Bruce Willis in all five films. An excellent character, an excellent role that undoubtedly brought Bruce Willis's career to a new level. It's a shame that we probably won't see him in this role anymore.

5. Ellen Ripley
"Alien"

In fifth place is the main heroine of the series of science fiction horror films "Alien" Ellen Louise Ripley. On the big screen, this character was played by American actress Sigourney Weaver, for whom this role was a huge breakthrough in her career, and not surprisingly, watch four films of the series and I am sure you will agree with this fact.

4. Batman
"DC and Marvel Cinematic Universe"

One of the characters of "Marvel" has already flown into this rating, let's give a chance to "DC". Well, Batman deserved this chance fully. A successful industrialist, philanthropist, lover of bats and cool high-tech equipment, who devoted his life to fighting for justice – and all this is one character. The role of Bruce Wayne was played by many actors, but as much as we twist, Batman was, is and will be one of the most cult movie heroes.

3. Han Solo
"Star Wars"

The top three leaders of our rating are opened by a smuggler, a spaceship pilot, a prince's husband and just an excellent guy (although with a difficult character) Han Solo. In the "Star Wars" saga, this role was played for many years by Harrison Ford, until in 2018, his young character was played by actor Alden Ehrenreich.

2. James Bond
"Bondiana"

Created by British writer Ian Fleming, the secret agent of MI-6, better known as Agent "007" James Bond, made his creator famous after the books were adapted into films. The adaptations were so successful that over the years there were as many as twenty-five "Bondiana" films, and the secret agent himself was played by eight actors at different times.

1. Indiana Jones
"Indiana Jones"

The first place and, I think, unexpectedly for many, is occupied by an archaeologist, scientist, doctor of historical sciences, intelligence officer, special agent and just a madly charismatic guy – Indiana Jones. Notably, his role, as well as the one occupying third place in this rating, Han Solo, was played by American actor Harrison Ford.
